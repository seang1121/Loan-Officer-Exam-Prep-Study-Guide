## Timelines & Deadlines

| Trigger | Deadline |
|---|---|
| Loan application received | Loan Estimate within **3 business days** |
| Before closing | Closing Disclosure at least **3 business days** prior |
| Right of rescission window | **3 business days** after consummation |
| Extended rescission (no disclosures) | Up to **3 years** |
| Adverse action notice (ECOA) | Within **30 days** of application decision |
| Adverse action notice (FCRA) | Within **30 days** of decision based on credit report |
| SAR filing (suspicious activity) | Within **30 days** of detection |
| CTR filing (cash transaction >$10K) | Within **15 days** |
| HMDA LAR filing | **March 1** of the following calendar year |
| Self-report conviction/financial event | Within **30 days** |
| Lock-in agreement expiration notification | Must notify borrower |

## Loan-to-Value (LTV) Rules

| Loan Type | Max LTV | PMI Required? |
|---|---|---|
| Conventional (no PMI) | 80% | No |
| Conventional (with PMI) | Up to 97% | Yes, until 80% LTV |
| PMI cancellation (automatic) | At **78% LTV** | Cancelled by lender |
| FHA (3.5% down) | 96.5% | MIP required |
| VA | 100% | No PMI (funding fee) |
| USDA | 100% | Guarantee fee instead |
