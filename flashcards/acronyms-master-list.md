# Acronyms Master List — NMLS SAFE Exam

**Every acronym you need to know, organized by category**

---

## FEDERAL LAWS & REGULATIONS

| Acronym | Full Name | What It Does |
|---|---|---|
| **TILA** | Truth in Lending Act | Discloses APR and cost of credit to borrowers |
| **RESPA** | Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act | Prohibits kickbacks; regulates settlement costs |
| **ECOA** | Equal Credit Opportunity Act | Prohibits credit discrimination (9 protected classes) |
| **HMDA** | Home Mortgage Disclosure Act | Requires mortgage data collection to detect redlining |
| **FCRA** | Fair Credit Reporting Act | Governs credit report use; dispute rights |
| **BSA** | Bank Secrecy Act | Anti-money laundering; requires CTR/SAR filing |
| **AML** | Anti-Money Laundering | Framework of laws combating money laundering (BSA core) |
| **CRA** | Community Reinvestment Act | Banks must lend in LMI communities |
| **HOEPA** | Home Ownership and Equity Protection Act | High-cost mortgage restrictions and disclosures |
| **SAFE** | Secure and Fair Enforcement (for Mortgage Licensing Act) | Created NMLS; requires MLO licensing |

## AGENCIES & ORGANIZATIONS

| Acronym | Full Name | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **CFPB** | Consumer Financial Protection Bureau | Enforces federal consumer financial laws; created by Dodd-Frank |
| **HUD** | Department of Housing and Urban Development | Oversees FHA, Ginnie Mae, Fair Housing enforcement |
| **VA** | Department of Veterans Affairs | Guarantees VA home loans for veterans |
| **USDA** | U.S. Department of Agriculture | USDA Rural Development loan program |
| **FHA** | Federal Housing Administration | Insures FHA loans (division of HUD) |
| **FHFA** | Federal Housing Finance Agency | Regulates FNMA, FHLMC, and FHLBs |
| **FNMA** | Federal National Mortgage Association | "Fannie Mae" — buys conventional conforming loans |
| **FHLMC** | Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corporation | "Freddie Mac" — buys conventional conforming loans |
| **GNMA** | Government National Mortgage Association | "Ginnie Mae" — guarantees MBS backed by FHA/VA/USDA |
| **NMLS** | National Mortgage Licensing System | Centralized licensing/registration for MLOs |
| **FinCEN** | Financial Crimes Enforcement Network | Treasury bureau; administers BSA; receives CTR/SARs |
| **OFAC** | Office of Foreign Assets Control | Maintains SDN list of sanctioned individuals/countries |
| **OCC** | Office of the Comptroller of the Currency | Regulates national banks |
| **FDIC** | Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation | Insures deposits; regulates state non-member banks |
| **FHLB** | Federal Home Loan Bank | Network of 12 regional banks providing liquidity to members |

## LOAN TYPES & PRODUCTS

| Acronym | Full Name | What It Is |
|---|---|---|
| **ARM** | Adjustable-Rate Mortgage | Rate adjusts periodically based on index + margin |
| **HECM** | Home Equity Conversion Mortgage | FHA-insured reverse mortgage for borrowers 62+ |
| **IRRRL** | Interest Rate Reduction Refinance Loan | VA streamline refinance (VA-to-VA only) |
| **HELOC** | Home Equity Line of Credit | Open-end revolving credit secured by home equity |
| **HEL** | Home Equity Loan | Closed-end second mortgage against home equity |
| **MBS** | Mortgage-Backed Securities | Pool of mortgages sold as investment securities |
| **CMO** | Collateralized Mortgage Obligation | MBS divided into tranches/risk levels |
| **GFE** | Good Faith Estimate | Old pre-TRID disclosure (replaced by LE) |

## MORTGAGE INSURANCE

| Acronym | Full Name | Used For |
|---|---|---|
| **MIP** | Mortgage Insurance Premium | FHA-insured loans (both UFMIP and annual) |
| **UFMIP** | Upfront Mortgage Insurance Premium | FHA upfront fee (1.75% of base loan) |
| **PMI** | Private Mortgage Insurance | Conventional loans with LTV > 80% |
| **MI** | Mortgage Insurance | Generic term for any mortgage insurance |
| **FHA MIP** | FHA Mortgage Insurance Premium | Specific to FHA loans (UFMIP + annual MIP) |

## UNDERWRITING & QUALIFICATION

| Acronym | Full Name | What It Is |
|---|---|---|
| **DTI** | Debt-to-Income Ratio | Monthly debt payments ÷ gross monthly income |
| **LTV** | Loan-to-Value Ratio | Loan amount ÷ appraised/purchase value |
| **CLTV** | Combined Loan-to-Value | All liens ÷ appraised value |
| **PITI** | Principal, Interest, Taxes, Insurance | Components of monthly housing payment |
| **AUS** | Automated Underwriting System | Software that evaluates loan applications |
| **DU** | Desktop Underwriter | Fannie Mae's AUS system |
| **LPA** | Loan Product Advisor | Freddie Mac's AUS (formerly Loan Prospector/LP) |
| **GUS** | Guaranteed Underwriting System | USDA's AUS system |
| **VOE** | Verification of Employment | Document confirming borrower's employment |
| **VOD** | Verification of Deposit | Document confirming bank account balances |
| **ROV** | Reconsideration of Value | Borrower's formal request to review appraisal value |
| **AMC** | Appraisal Management Company | Manages appraiser assignments; creates independence |
| **FICO** | Fair Isaac Corporation | Credit scoring model; the most common credit score |

## DISCLOSURES & DOCUMENTS

| Acronym | Full Name | What It Is |
|---|---|---|
| **LE** | Loan Estimate | 3-page disclosure replacing GFE (TRID); due within 3 business days |
| **CD** | Closing Disclosure | 5-page disclosure replacing HUD-1; due 3 business days before closing |
| **TRID** | TILA-RESPA Integrated Disclosure | "Know Before You Owe" — rules governing LE and CD |
| **AfBA** | Affiliated Business Arrangement | When a referrer has financial interest in referred settlement service |
| **APR** | Annual Percentage Rate | True cost of credit including interest + fees, expressed annually |
| **TIP** | Total Interest Percentage | Total interest paid over life of loan as % of loan amount (on LE/CD Page 3) |
| **LAR** | Loan Application Register | HMDA annual data file; filed by March 1 |
| **URLA** | Uniform Residential Loan Application | Standard mortgage application form (Form 1003) |
| **MSA** | Metropolitan Statistical Area | Urban area defined by Census; relevant for HMDA reporting |

| Acronym | Full Name | What It Is |
|---|---|---|
| **CTR** | Currency Transaction Report | Filed for cash transactions >$10,000; due within 15 days |
| **SAR** | Suspicious Activity Report | Filed for suspected illegal activity; due within 30 days |
| **CIP** | Customer Identification Program | PATRIOT Act requirement to verify customer identity |
| **KYC** | Know Your Customer | Due diligence process to verify customer identity and assess risk |
| **CDD** | Customer Due Diligence | 5th pillar of AML program; identify beneficial owners |

## HIGH-COST & HIGHER-PRICED

| Acronym | Full Name | What It Is |
|---|---|---|
| **APOR** | Average Prime Offer Rate | Benchmark rate published weekly by CFPB; used for HOEPA/HPML thresholds |
| **HPML** | Higher-Priced Mortgage Loan | Loan with APR > APOR + 1.5% (1st lien); requires escrow + appraisal |
| **APR** | Annual Percentage Rate | Rate used to compare to APOR for HPML/HOEPA determination |

## QUICK QUIZ — TEST YOURSELF

**Without looking above, match these:**

1. DU → ?
2. LPA → ?
3. GNMA → ?
4. UFMIP → ?
5. APOR → ?
6. IRRRL → ?
7. AfBA → ?
8. GUS → ?
9. CTR → ?
10. SAR → ?

**Answers:**
1. DU → Desktop Underwriter (Fannie Mae's AUS)
2. LPA → Loan Product Advisor (Freddie Mac's AUS)
3. GNMA → Ginnie Mae (guarantees MBS, doesn't buy loans)
4. UFMIP → Upfront Mortgage Insurance Premium (FHA: 1.75%)
5. APOR → Average Prime Offer Rate (published weekly by CFPB)
6. IRRRL → Interest Rate Reduction Refinance Loan (VA streamline)
7. AfBA → Affiliated Business Arrangement (disclose at time of referral)
8. GUS → Guaranteed Underwriting System (USDA's AUS)
9. CTR → Currency Transaction Report (cash >$10K)
10. SAR → Suspicious Activity Report (suspicious activity; 30 days)
