### 1. The SAFE Act & NMLS

**SAFE Act (Secure and Fair Enforcement for Mortgage Licensing Act ‚Äî 2008)**
- Created a **nationwide licensing system** for MLOs ‚Üí NMLS
- Required ALL MLOs to register or be licensed
- Background checks, credit checks, education requirements
- **Bank employees** ‚Üí register (not license) with NMLS
- **Non-bank MLOs** (brokers, independent lenders) ‚Üí must be **state-licensed**

**NMLS Requirements to Get Licensed:**
- 20 hours of pre-licensing education
  - 3 hrs federal law
  - 3 hrs ethics (fraud, consumer protection, fair lending)
  - 2 hrs non-traditional mortgage products
  - 12 hrs electives
- Pass the SAFE exam (75%+)
- Background check + credit check
- Surety bond (amount varies by state)

**Annual Renewal:**
- 8 hours of continuing education (CE)
  - 3 hrs federal law
  - 2 hrs ethics
  - 2 hrs non-traditional mortgage products
  - 1 hr elective

> ‚ö° **Exam Tip:** The SAFE Act created the NMLS. Bank employees REGISTER. Non-bank MLOs get LICENSED. This distinction comes up.

### 2. Prohibited Acts (KNOW THESE COLD)

| Prohibited Act | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **Steering** | Directing a borrower to a loan that isn't in their best interest (e.g., higher rate because YOU get more commission) |
| **Redlining** | Refusing to lend in certain geographic areas based on race/ethnicity |
| **Reverse redlining** | Targeting minority neighborhoods with predatory loan products |
| **Bait and switch** | Advertising one rate/product, then switching to worse terms at closing |
| **Kickbacks** | Accepting or paying for loan referrals (RESPA ¬ß8 violation) |
| **Misrepresentation** | Falsifying or omitting material information on a loan application |
| **Churning** | Repeatedly refinancing a borrower just to generate fees |

---

### 3. Fair Lending Laws

**Fair Housing Act ‚Äî 7 Protected Classes:**
1. Race
2. Color
3. National Origin
4. Religion
5. Sex
6. Familial Status (families with children under 18, pregnant women)
7. Handicap/Disability

**ECOA ‚Äî Additional Protected Classes:**
- Age
- Marital Status
- Receipt of public assistance income
- Exercise of rights under Consumer Protection Act

> ‚ö° **Exam Tip:** Fair Housing = 7 classes. ECOA adds age, marital status, income source. Both laws can apply to mortgage transactions.

---

### 4. MLO Advertising Ethics

Must include on ALL advertising:
- Your **NMLS ID number**
- Your **company name** as registered

Cannot do:
- Advertise rates without proper disclosures (Reg Z triggers)
- Use the word "free" for something that isn't
- Imply government affiliation (e.g., "FHA-approved lender" misleadingly)
- Omit required disclosures triggered by a specific rate mention

---

### 5. Financial Responsibility

MLOs must:
- Self-report any criminal conviction within 30 days
- Self-report any financial event (bankruptcy, judgment) within 30 days
- Maintain good credit standing as a condition of licensure

**Q: What does SAFE stand for?**
A: Secure and Fair Enforcement for Mortgage Licensing Act

**Q: What year was the SAFE Act passed?**
A: 2008

**Q: How many hours of pre-licensing education does NMLS require?**
A: 20 hours

**Q: How many hours of CE are required annually?**
A: 8 hours

**Q: What is steering?**
A: Directing a borrower toward a loan product that benefits the MLO rather than the borrower

**Q: What is redlining?**
A: Refusing to make loans in specific geographic areas based on race or ethnicity

**Q: How many protected classes does the Fair Housing Act cover?**
A: 7 (Race, Color, National Origin, Religion, Sex, Familial Status, Disability)

**Q: What additional protected classes does ECOA add?**
A: Age, Marital Status, Public Assistance Income, Exercise of consumer protection rights

**Q: What must always appear in MLO advertising?**
A: NMLS ID number and company name

**Q: What is churning?**
A: Repeatedly refinancing a borrower's loan to generate fees without benefit to the borrower

**Q: Bank employees who take mortgage applications must ___. Non-bank MLOs must ___.**
A: Register with NMLS | Be state-licensed through NMLS
