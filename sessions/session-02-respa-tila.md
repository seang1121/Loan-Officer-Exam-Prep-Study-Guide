## RESPA (Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act) ‚Äî Reg X

**Purpose:** Protects borrowers from abusive practices in the settlement process ‚Äî specifically kickbacks, referral fees, and inflated settlement costs.

### Key RESPA Provisions

**Section 8 ‚Äî Kickbacks (MOST TESTED)**
- Prohibits paying or receiving fees for referrals of settlement services
- Settlement services = title, appraisal, insurance, closing services, mortgage
- **Exception:** Bona fide services actually performed CAN be compensated
- **Penalty:** Up to 1 year imprisonment + fine up to $10,000

> ‚ö° **Exam Tip:** A title company giving an MLO a "marketing" fee = RESPA ¬ß8 violation. No referral fees. Period.

**Section 9 ‚Äî Title Insurance**
- Seller CANNOT require buyer to use a specific title insurance company as a condition of sale

**Section 10 ‚Äî Escrow Accounts**
- Limits how much lenders can require in escrow cushion
- Max cushion: **1/6 of estimated annual disbursements** (2 months)
- Annual escrow analysis required

**RESPA Disclosures:**

| Disclosure | When Required | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Loan Estimate (LE) | Replaced GFE | Within 3 business days of application |
| Closing Disclosure (CD) | Replaced HUD-1 | 3 business days BEFORE closing |
| Servicing Disclosure | At application | Within 3 business days |
| Affiliated Business Arrangement (AfBA) | When referring to affiliate | At time of referral |

**What triggers a RESPA application?**
- 6 pieces of info = application:
  1. Name
  2. Income
  3. SSN (for credit check)
  4. Property address
  5. Estimated property value
  6. Loan amount desired

> ‚ö° **Exam Tip:** Once all 6 items are collected = application exists. LE must be issued within 3 business days.

## TILA (Truth in Lending Act) ‚Äî Reg Z

**Purpose:** Requires lenders to disclose the TRUE cost of credit so borrowers can comparison shop.

### Key TILA Requirements

**APR (Annual Percentage Rate)**
- Must be disclosed
- Includes interest rate + fees expressed as annual rate
- More accurate reflection of loan cost than the note rate

**APR Tolerance (if APR changes between LE and CD):**
- Regular loans: APR cannot change by more than **0.125%**
- Irregular loans: APR cannot change by more than **0.25%**
- If it does ‚Üí new LE must be issued, new 3-day waiting period

**Right of Rescission**
- Applies to: Refinances and home equity loans on PRIMARY residence
- Does NOT apply to: Purchase transactions
- Borrower has **3 business days** after consummation to rescind
- Extended rescission: Up to **3 years** if proper disclosures not provided

> ‚ö° **Exam Tip:** No right of rescission on a purchase! Only refinances/HELOCs on primary residence.

**TRID (TILA-RESPA Integrated Disclosure Rule)**
Merged RESPA and TILA disclosures into two forms:
- **Loan Estimate (LE)** ‚Äî 3 pages, given within 3 business days of application
- **Closing Disclosure (CD)** ‚Äî 5 pages, given 3 business days before closing

**Loan Estimate Tolerances:**

| Fee Category | Tolerance |
|---|---|
| Origination charges | 0% ‚Äî cannot increase at all |
| Required services (no shopping) | 0% |
| Required services (can shop, used lender list) | 10% aggregate |
| Prepaid interest, insurance premiums | No tolerance limit |
| Services NOT required by lender | No tolerance limit |

**Higher-Priced Mortgage Loans (HPML)**
- Rate is ‚â• 1.5% above APOR for first lien
- Rate is ‚â• 3.5% above APOR for subordinate lien
- **Requirements:** Mandatory escrow (5 years), appraisal required, no prepayment penalty

**High-Cost Mortgage (HOEPA)**
- Rate is ‚â• 6.5% above APOR (first lien)
- Points and fees ‚â• 5% of loan amount
- **Restrictions:** No balloon payments (in most cases), no negative amortization, mandatory counseling

**LO Compensation Rule (Reg Z)**
- MLO compensation CANNOT be based on loan terms (rate, points, APR)
- MLO CAN be compensated based on loan amount
- Dual compensation prohibited: cannot receive pay from both borrower and lender on same loan

## üÉè PART 2: FLASHCARDS

**Q: What does RESPA stand for?**
A: Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act

**Q: What does RESPA Section 8 prohibit?**
A: Kickbacks and unearned referral fees for settlement services

**Q: What replaced the Good Faith Estimate (GFE)?**
A: Loan Estimate (LE)

**Q: What replaced the HUD-1 Settlement Statement?**
A: Closing Disclosure (CD)

**Q: How many business days after application must the LE be delivered?**
A: 3 business days

**Q: How many business days before closing must the CD be delivered?**
A: 3 business days

**Q: What are the 6 pieces of info that trigger a RESPA application?**
A: Name, Income, SSN, Property Address, Estimated Value, Loan Amount

**Q: What is the right of rescission?**
A: Borrower's right to cancel a refinance or HELOC on their primary residence within 3 business days of consummation

**Q: Does right of rescission apply to purchase transactions?**
A: No ‚Äî only refinances and home equity loans on primary residence

**Q: What is TRID?**
A: TILA-RESPA Integrated Disclosure Rule ‚Äî merged GFE/HUD-1 into LE/CD

**Q: What is an HPML (Higher-Priced Mortgage Loan)?**
A: First lien loan with APR ‚â• 1.5% above APOR; requires mandatory escrow and appraisal

**Q: Can MLO compensation be based on the interest rate of the loan?**
A: No ‚Äî LO Comp Rule prohibits compensation tied to loan terms

**Q: What is the maximum escrow cushion allowed under RESPA?**
A: 1/6 of estimated annual disbursements (approximately 2 months)

**Q: What is the APR tolerance for regular loans under TILA?**
A: 0.125%
